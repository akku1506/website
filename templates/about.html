About Me

I am an Applied Scientist 2 at Amazon, where my work focuses on better representation learning for multimodal models and utilizing diffusion models. These days, I have been working on improving the visual cues of vision-language models. In particular, multimodal models hallucinate, and one of the reasons for hallucination is the lack of visual grounding. My research pursuits lie in the field of Machine learning, Computer vision, and at the intersection of Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing. Previously, I was a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India, working on generating SQL from text, mentored by Dr. Arun Iyer. I completed my Master's under the supervision of Dr. Narayanan C Krishnanan at IIT Ropar. Apart from spending my time doing research, I hold a keen interest in dance and yoga that I believe bring tranquility in my life.

Besides my current experiences, I am always open to exploring new challenges and problems presented to me in this domain.
